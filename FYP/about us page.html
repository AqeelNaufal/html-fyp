Welcome To Our Website

We are a group of dedicated students from Polytechnic Ungku Omar, currently in our final year of the Diploma in Digital Technology (DDTS6) program. As part of our educational journey, we have embarked on an exciting and innovative project.

Our Project: CYBERWAREEDUGAME

Our final year project, titled "CYBERWARE EDU GAME," is a web-based game that represents the culmination of our efforts and knowledge gained throughout our program. This educational game is designed to make learning about digital technology and practice defense strategies against cybercrime. Through CYBERWARE EDU GAME, we aim to inspire a passion for technology in our peers and those who explore our creation.
